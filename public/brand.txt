## 4. Role-Based Architecture

FindOO enforces strict role-based access control aligned with regulatory status:

- **Issuers** (AMCs, NBFCs, Insurance Companies, Banks): Can list and showcase regulated financial products. Cannot provide individual advice.
- **Intermediaries** (MFDs, RIAs, Insurance Agents, CAs, CFAs): Can publish Opinions classified as Education or Sentiment Signal. Cannot issue buy/sell recommendations.
- **Investors** (Individual and Institutional): Can post Requirements (pull-based intent signals). Cannot publish financial opinions or tips.

This role-based separation is regulatory architecture — not a product feature.

## 5. Voice & Tone

### Personality
- **Authoritative** but approachable — like a trusted industry insider
- **Neutral** — never promotional of specific products, professionals, or firms
- **Clear** — no jargon soup; explain complex concepts simply
- **Confident** — state facts, don't hedge unnecessarily

### Language Rules
- Use "financial network" not "social media for finance"
- Use "verified professionals" not "users"
- Use "Opinions" (capitalised) for the platform's sentiment feature — never "polls" or "surveys"
- Use "Showcase" not "marketplace" or "directory" for listings
- Use "Requirements" for investor feed posts — never "tips" or "recommendations"
- Refer to the three user pillars: **Issuers, Intermediaries & Investors**

### Content Classification
All user-generated content is classified under one of three intent tags:
- **Education**: Awareness and educational content for the BFSI community
- **Sentiment Signal**: Aggregated professional sentiment on policy, markets, and regulation — explicitly not investment advice
- **Awareness**: Industry discussions and macro-level awareness content

### Compliance Language
- Never imply FindOO provides financial advice
- Never position FindOO as an intermediary or broker
- Always include: "FindOO is a neutral infrastructure provider. It does not provide financial advice."
- Opinions are "aggregated sentiment signals" — not predictions or recommendations

## 7. Core Features

- **Financial Network Feed**: Role-gated content publishing with compliance classification
- **Verified Opinions**: Aggregated sentiment signals from verified BFSI professionals (Education / Sentiment Signal / Awareness)
- **Professional Directory**: Public directory of AMFI-registered and SEBI-verified professionals
- **Jobs Board**: Financial-sector specific listings (Fund Management, Compliance, Wealth Advisory, Risk, Actuarial)
- **Events**: Webinars, investor meets, AGM/EGM, NFO/IPO launches, earnings calls
- **Showcase**: Discover and list regulated financial products (Mutual Funds, Insurance, PMS, AIF, Bonds) and services (Advisory, Compliance, Tax Planning)
- **Digital Business Cards**: Verified vCard with QR code sharing — credentials included
- **Vault**: Secure document storage for financial professionals
- **Verification System**: Identity and credential verification via SEBI/RBI/IRDAI/AMFI registries
- **Network**: Role-aware connections between Issuers, Intermediaries, and Investors
- **Gamification**: XP system, streaks, badges, leaderboard, and weekly challenges
- **Compare**: Side-by-side comparisons with social networks, professional networks, and financial media

## 8. Neutrality & Data Provenance

- **Non-Intermediary Status**: FindOO does not earn commissions on products or provide individual investment advice
- **Verified-at-Source Data**: 100% of professional users are verified at onboarding against public regulatory registries
- **Signal Attribution**: Opinions are aggregated sentiment signals; individual professionals may remain publicly anonymous, but credentials are cryptographically linked for regulatory auditability
- **Investor Intent Restriction**: Investor feed activity is limited to "Requirements" (pull-based marketing) to prevent unverified tips and market manipulation

## 10. Crawler & Discoverability Strategy

### Search & AI Crawlers
FindOO explicitly permits indexing by major search and AI crawlers (Googlebot, Bingbot, GPTBot, ClaudeBot, PerplexityBot, Google-Extended, cohere-ai) with the following restrictions:
- **Private routes blocked**: /vault, /messages, /settings, /auth
- **Default crawlers** additionally restricted from: /feed, /network, /bookmarks, /discover, /leaderboard

### Social Preview Crawlers
Twitterbot, facebookexternalhit, LinkedInBot, and WhatsApp are allowed full access for rich link previews.

### Key Crawler Files
- `robots.txt`: https://findoo.in/robots.txt
- `sitemap.xml`: https://findoo.in/sitemap.xml
- `llms.txt`: https://findoo.in/llms.txt (AI crawler context — platform role, compliance, features)
- `brand.txt`: https://findoo.in/brand.txt (this file)
