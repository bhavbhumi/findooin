### Decorative & Background Elements

#### Inner-Page Heroes (PageHero Component)
Every inner page hero uses a subtle animated geometric decoration matched to content type via a `variant` prop:

| Variant | Shape | Best Used For |
|---------|-------|---------------|
| circles (default) | Concentric rings, dots, gradient blob | General pages (About, Privacy, Terms) |
| squares | Rotated rectangles, small diamond accents | Structured content (Compare, Sitemap, Legal) |
| triangles | Outlined triangles, small rotating accent | Directional pages (Support, Contact, Pricing) |
| hexagons | Nested hexagons, pulsing dot | Technical pages (Developer Docs, API Reference) |
| waves | Layered sine curves, gradient wipe | Flowing content (Blog, Events, Opinions) |
| dots | Grid of dots, bordered circles | Data-driven pages (Directory, Jobs, Leaderboard) |

All decorations:
- Use `primary` and `accent` semantic tokens only — never raw colours
- Render at 8–20% opacity — visible but never dominant
- Animate via framer-motion on mount (scale, fade, rotate)
- Include one pulsing accent dot for subtle life
- Include a blurred gradient blob (blur-3xl) for depth

#### Landing Page Sections (SectionDecorations)
Global-level decorative elements used across Landing and feature sections:

| Component | Type | Usage |
|-----------|------|-------|
| NetworkMesh | SVG pattern (grid + diagonals) | Hero backgrounds — represents interconnected BFSI mesh |
| DotGrid | SVG dot pattern | Subtle texture overlay for empty sections |
| GlowBlob | Blurred CSS gradient | Depth and ambient colour — positioned at section corners/center |
| Sparkles | Animated floating dots | Hero image accents — 5 pulsing dots |
| ConcentricRings | SVG concentric circles | Corner decoration for feature sections |
| DiamondGrid | SVG rotated squares | Geometric texture for testimonials/social proof |

#### Decoration Rules
- All decorative elements are `pointer-events-none` and `aria-hidden="true"`
- Never cover or compete with content — always `absolute` positioned behind content
- Dark mode inherits opacity from semantic tokens — no separate dark overrides needed
- Maximum two GlowBlobs per section to avoid visual clutter

## 4. Role-Based Architecture

FindOO enforces strict role-based access control aligned with regulatory status:

- **Issuers** (AMCs, NBFCs, Insurance Companies, Banks): Can list and showcase regulated financial products. Cannot provide individual advice.
- **Intermediaries** (MFDs, RIAs, Insurance Agents, CAs, CFAs): Can publish Opinions classified as Education or Sentiment Signal. Cannot issue buy/sell recommendations.
- **Investors** (Individual and Institutional): Can post Requirements (pull-based intent signals). Cannot publish financial opinions or tips.

This role-based separation is regulatory architecture — not a product feature.

## 6. Voice Rules

| Dimension | Do | Don't |
|-----------|-----|-------|
| Authority | State facts confidently | Hedge or over-qualify |
| Neutrality | Present all sides equally | Favour any product, firm, or professional |
| Clarity | Explain complex concepts simply | Use jargon soup or acronym chains without context |
| Warmth | Be approachable and human | Be robotic, overly formal, or corporate-speak |
| Humour | Subtle, dry, industry-aware wit | Memes, slang, Gen-Z tone, or sarcasm |
| Urgency | Never manufacture FOMO | Use "act now", "limited time", "don't miss out" |

### Terminology Map (always use left column)
- "Financial network" — not "social media for finance"
- "Verified professionals" — not "users"
- "Opinions" (capitalised) — not "polls" or "surveys"
- "Showcase" — not "marketplace" or "directory"
- "Requirements" — not "tips" or "recommendations"
- "Issuers, Intermediaries & Investors" — the three pillars, always in this order

### Content Classification
All user-generated content is classified under one of three intent tags:
- **Education**: Awareness and educational content for the BFSI community
- **Sentiment Signal**: Aggregated professional sentiment on policy, markets, and regulation — explicitly not investment advice
- **Awareness**: Industry discussions and macro-level awareness content

### Compliance Language
- Never imply FindOO provides financial advice
- Never position FindOO as an intermediary or broker
- Always include: "FindOO is a neutral infrastructure provider. It does not provide financial advice."
- Opinions are "aggregated sentiment signals" — not predictions or recommendations

## 8. Mood Board Directives

### Visual Mood
Institutional gravitas meets modern tech — think Bloomberg Terminal aesthetics crossed with a premium fintech app.

| Mood Attribute | Direction | Reference Points |
|---------------|-----------|-----------------|
| Palette feel | Deep, anchored, confident | Midnight Blue (#00008A) grounds; Gold (#D4AF37) elevates; Teal (#2DBFA5) activates |
| Photography style | If used — real professionals in real settings, natural light, no stock-photo smiles. Prefer candid over posed | Avoid: generic handshake photos, laptop-in-café, diverse-team-around-table clichés |
| Illustration style | Geometric, minimal, line-based — never cartoonish or 3D-rendered | Constellation/network motifs, orbital diagrams, subtle grid patterns |
| Iconography | Lucide icon set, consistent stroke weight, semantic colour only | Never: emoji, filled icons with gradients, or custom illustrated icons |
| Motion | Purposeful, restrained, physics-based (spring easing) | Mount animations, subtle parallax, pulsing accents — never bouncy, flashy, or attention-seeking |
| Texture | Subtle geometric patterns (dot grids, network mesh, concentric rings) at 3–20% opacity | Never: noise textures, paper textures, or skeuomorphic surfaces |
| Whitespace | Generous — let content breathe | Financial data is dense; the UI must counterbalance with spatial relief |
| Dark mode | Default/hero presentation — deep navy (#0F1528), not pure black | Glassmorphism accents (backdrop-blur-sm), elevated card shadows, semi-transparent hover states |
| Imagery hierarchy | Data > Diagrams > Icons > Photography | The platform sells information architecture, not lifestyle |

- **Digital Business Cards**: Verified vCard with QR code sharing — credentials included
- **Vault**: Secure document storage for financial professionals
- **Verification System**: Identity and credential verification via SEBI/RBI/IRDAI/AMFI registries
- **Network**: Role-aware connections between Issuers, Intermediaries, and Investors
- **Gamification**: XP system, streaks, badges, leaderboard, and weekly challenges
- **Compare**: Side-by-side comparisons with social networks, professional networks, and financial media

## 11. Neutrality & Data Provenance

- **Non-Intermediary Status**: FindOO does not earn commissions on products or provide individual investment advice
- **Verified-at-Source Data**: 100% of professional users are verified at onboarding against public regulatory registries
- **Signal Attribution**: Opinions are aggregated sentiment signals; individual professionals may remain publicly anonymous, but credentials are cryptographically linked for regulatory auditability
- **Investor Intent Restriction**: Investor feed activity is limited to "Requirements" (pull-based marketing) to prevent unverified tips and market manipulation

## 13. Crawler & Discoverability Strategy

### Search & AI Crawlers
FindOO explicitly permits indexing by major search and AI crawlers (Googlebot, Bingbot, GPTBot, ClaudeBot, PerplexityBot, Google-Extended, cohere-ai) with the following restrictions:
- **Private routes blocked**: /vault, /messages, /settings, /auth
- **Default crawlers** additionally restricted from: /feed, /network, /bookmarks, /discover, /leaderboard

### Social Preview Crawlers
Twitterbot, facebookexternalhit, LinkedInBot, and WhatsApp are allowed full access for rich link previews.

### Key Crawler Files
- `robots.txt`: https://findoo.in/robots.txt
- `sitemap.xml`: https://findoo.in/sitemap.xml
- `llms.txt`: https://findoo.in/llms.txt (AI crawler context — platform role, compliance, features)
- `brand.txt`: https://findoo.in/brand.txt (this file)
